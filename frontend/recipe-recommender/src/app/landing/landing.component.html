Meal Mate aims to provide users with personalized recipes to help with discovering new meals based on their taste preferences and skill level in the kitchen. Users will initially filter recipes by taking a survey that gauges the users taste preferences, the time they have to spend on the recipe, and the ingredients they have to give personalized recommendations to that user.
